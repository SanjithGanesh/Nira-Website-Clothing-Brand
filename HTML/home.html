About NIRA

Mission

To weave timeless elegance and comfort into every saree, while empowering local artisans and preserving the rich heritage of Indian textiles for modern lifestyles.

Vision

To become a globally cherished symbol of sustainable fashion, bridging tradition and innovation, and inspiring a love for handcrafted, ethical, and lasting beauty.

Nira is not just a saree brand—it is a celebration of India’s rich textile heritage, reimagined for today’s world. Each saree combines the charm of traditional craftsmanship with the practicality of modern-day washing and water quality standards, ensuring timeless durability and unmatched comfort.

At its heart, Nira works closely with local artisans, preserving the essence of Indian fabrics while championing ethical practices. Every drape tells a story of care & resilience.

About the Founder

A lockdown hobby turned revolution. Sandhia started Nira at her kitchen table, armed with a laptop, endless chai, and a mission to prove that sarees can be both comfortable and culturally rich.

When she’s not nerding out over Mughal-era block prints or testing fabric durability, she’s probably convincing her grandma that TikTok is ‘the new radio.’

Her mantra? ‘If it can’t survive a washing machine, it doesn’t deserve to call itself heritage.’
